Top Destinations in Myanmar

Bagan

See ancient temples and pagodas. Watch beautiful sunsets and sunrises. Enjoy hot air balloon rides for stunning views!

Inle Lake

Enjoy boat rides on the lake. Visit local markets and see unique floating gardens. Meet local fishermen using their legs to paddle!

Yangon

Visit the famous Shwedagon Pagoda. Explore lively markets and try delicious street food. Experience the city's rich culture!

Mandalay

Climb Mandalay Hill for great views. See ancient royal palaces and temples. Enjoy local arts and crafts!

Ngapali Beach

Relax on beautiful sandy beaches. Swim in clear waters and enjoy fresh seafood. Great for sunbathing and beach games!

Bago

See the huge reclining Buddha. Visit ancient pagodas and learn about local history. Enjoy peaceful surroundings!

Hpa-An

Explore beautiful caves and mountains. Enjoy nature walks and visit local villages. A great place for hiking and relaxing!

Kyaiktiyo (Golden Rock)

See the famous Golden Rock perched on the edge of a cliff. Enjoy breathtaking views and spiritual experiences!

Mrauk U

Visit ancient ruins and learn about the Arakan Kingdom. Enjoy unique cultural experiences in a quiet setting!

Pyin Oo Lwin

Enjoy cool weather and beautiful gardens. Visit the famous National Gardens and explore local markets!
